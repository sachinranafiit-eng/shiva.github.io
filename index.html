Shiva Enterprises is a Dehradun-based technical engineering and infrastructure solutions company founded by Sachin Kumar Rana, a Senior Project MEP Professional with more than 21 years of multidisciplinary experience in electrical engineering, facility management, IT infrastructure, low-voltage systems, project execution, technical operations, and institutional development.

Over the course of his career, Sachin has successfully contributed to and managed projects across residential townships, commercial developments, healthcare facilities, educational institutions, corporate offices, industrial facilities, and smart city infrastructure programs.
